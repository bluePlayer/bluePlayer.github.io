Wonders in Math Land

Having the latest version of your browser is always recommended. To play this game your browser must support HTML5 features. If you cannot view the game or it doesn't work, refer to this list of supported browsers:

  • Google Chrome, latest version(it installs updates automatically)
  • Firefox 34 or better
  • Opera 26 or better
  • Safari 5 or better(doesnt' play HTML5 audio if there is no QuickTime installed on the client computer)
  • Internet Explorer 9 or better
This is Alpha version 4.1.1 of the game. It will evolve depending on the interest about it.
